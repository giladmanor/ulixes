Bringing Science to Marketing.

Ulixes.io is the unified solution for your Loyalty, Gamification and Marketing Automation needs, and the best thing about it: it’s open source!

Ulixes is built for marketers, product guys (and girls) and growth hackers, and allows you to easily respond to user actions without having to wait for the lazy developers to finally implement your genius ideas.

Ulixes allows you to:

  • Cluster users and prospects based on user behavior
  • Define rules of engagement for users
  • Set up personalized Messaging
  • Personalized call-to-action
  • Grant and revoke Badges
  • Move users between Levels
  • Grant specific permissions per Level
